Welcome to Chickencraft

Chickencraft is a Minecraft with non-toxic community and friendly staff, we support both java & bedrock. Our server varies in different popular gamemodes such as lifesteal, pvp, boxpvp and bedwars. Chickencraft supports European, and Asian users offering low ping to our players. Server has heigh moderation, fun events and purchasable ranks. Why us? Chickencrafft will offer you the best playtime experience you will find new friends to play with and over all enjoy your stay on our server we also have server updates and new features every week to keep you guys occupied.

Gamemodes

Lifesteal Smp

Lifesteal is where people fight against each other to steal ones heart. If you kill someone you steal one of their hearts if you die you lose a heart.

BoxPvP

BoxPVP is PVP based gamemode where people grind to get op gear in a small box shaped map with fun minigames to earn rewords.

PVP

Pvp practise is a good way to improve pvp skills battle your friends with different kits, enjoy free for all battles or pvp against a coded pvp practise bots to test your skills.

Advanced Survival

Survival is a fun gamemode where you can team up with others or play alone to become the richest player in the server and build op farms to earn money and build cool mantions with your friends without it being distroyed by others.

Bedwars

BedWars is a 1.8.9 gamemode where one's objective is to break other peoples beds and prevent them from respawning last man standing wins. You can play solos, duos and squads.

Parkour

Parkour is a fun game where you test your skills by jumping along different obsticals to reach your goal, with over 40 maps and three different levels.
